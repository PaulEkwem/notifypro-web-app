Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026  ·  NotifyPro Nigeria Ltd, Gbagada, Lagos

NotifyPro ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our platform at notifypro.ng.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email address, business name, and password when you sign up.
  • Usage data: SMS/email campaigns you create, delivery logs, contact lists, and API activity.
  • Billing data: wallet top-up history and transaction records.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information collected automatically.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the NotifyPro platform.
  • To send you transactional emails (account verification, password reset, billing receipts).
  • To process your SMS/email campaigns on your behalf.
  • To improve platform performance and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with applicable Nigerian and international laws.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers: SMS gateways, email delivery services, and payment processors necessary to operate the platform.
  • Legal authorities: when required by Nigerian law or a valid court order.

4. Your Contacts' Data

When you upload contact lists to NotifyPro, you confirm that you have lawful permission to message those individuals. You are the data controller for your contacts. We process their data solely on your instructions.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Delivery logs are retained for 12 months. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing privacy@notifypro.ng.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest), access controls, and regular security audits. However, no system is completely secure — please use a strong password and keep your API keys confidential.

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children

NotifyPro is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you by email or a banner on the dashboard for material changes.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@notifypro.ng or write to us at our office in Gbagada, Lagos, Nigeria.
